Hi Team,

I'm excited to share **CAPD** — a new mobile-first web app built for ISB that brings attendance, events, and notifications together in one place.

## What is CAPD?

CAPD is a secure, cohort-aware platform that helps us:
- **Track Attendance** with rotating 6-digit codes (changes every 45 seconds — impossible to predict)
- **Manage Events** with full descriptions, targeted to specific cohorts or custom groups
- **Send Notifications** instantly to students across all 4 cohorts
- **Keep Data Private** — each cohort only sees their own information

## Key Features

✓ Institutional email + PIN login  
✓ Push notifications for instant updates  
✓ Works on mobile (PWA), tablet, and desktop  
✓ Secure & scalable (built on Supabase + Vercel)  
✓ Admin-friendly (no coding needed)
